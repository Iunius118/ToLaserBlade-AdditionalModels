# Cheatsheet

ToLaserBlade-ExtraModels v0.1.0

This mod is a ToLaserBlade add-on to add more laser blade shapes.

## Requirements

- Forge
  - ToLaserBlade (Forge) v11.0.0+
  - https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/tolaserblade
  - https://modrinth.com/mod/tolaserblade
- NeoForge
  - ToLaserBlade (NeoForge) v11.0.0+
  - https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/tolaserblade
  - https://modrinth.com/mod/tolaserblade
- Fabric
  - ToLaserBlade (Fabric) v6.0.0+
  - https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/tolaserblade-fabric
